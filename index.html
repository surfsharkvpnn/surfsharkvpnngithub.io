Frequently Asked Questions

Is creating a Surfshark account free?

Creating an account is part of the subscription process. You choose a plan during registration.

Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Yes. Surfshark allows unlimited simultaneous device connections under one account.

Can I change my password later?

Yes. You can update your account password from your account settings after signing in.

What should I do if I forget my password?
