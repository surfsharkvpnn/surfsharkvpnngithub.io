How to Login to Your Surfshark Account

Signing in to your Surfshark account takes only a few minutes. Ensure you have your registered email address and password before starting the login process.

  1. Open the Surfshark application or website.
  2. Click the Log In button.
  3. Enter your registered email address.
  4. Type your account password.
  5. Click the Sign In button.
  6. Your Surfshark dashboard will open successfully.

Common Surfshark Login Issues

If you cannot access your account, verify your email address and password, check your internet connection, update the Surfshark app, clear your browser cache, or reset your password if you've forgotten it.

Security Tips

  • Keep the Surfshark application updated.
  • Sign out from public or shared devices.
  • Regularly review your account activity.
